Is my mask safe to use for COVID-19?

During the current COVID-19 pandemic, foreign retailers, distributors and manufacturers of respirator and surgical masks sell fake, unsafe or uncertified masks all over the world. This application helps you to quickly analyze your face mask and to check if is a genuine safe product that will protect you from the corona virus.

Check your mask

Take a picture of your mask to verify if it is safe to use during the COVID-19 pandemic. The analysis will run completely in the browser and will not store any pictures on our server.
